About INTEGRITY Capital

INTEGRITY Capital is an independent investment firm with a global outlook and a research-led approach to long-term capital allocation.

Who We Are

The firm combines experience across investment research, cross-border market analysis, digital asset strategy, and capital allocation. We focus on identifying opportunities where disciplined analysis and selective positioning may produce attractive long-term outcomes.

How We Think

We emphasize business quality, earnings durability, downside awareness, and structural growth themes. Our process favors clarity of thesis, realistic scenario analysis, and risk-adjusted return thinking over short-term market noise.

What We Value

  • Research discipline
  • Long-term capital preservation
  • Selective conviction rather than excessive activity
  • Cross-market perspective across the US and Asia
