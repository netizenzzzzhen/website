Investment Scope

We allocate capital across selected global opportunities with focus on liquidity, structural growth, and differentiated return potential.

Global Public Markets

We invest in listed equities across the United States, Asia, and other selected regions. Our focus includes technology leaders, large-cap quality businesses, sector winners, and selected dislocation-driven opportunities.

Digital Asset Strategies

We maintain dedicated strategies for virtual assets, including major cryptocurrencies, infrastructure-related themes, and selected opportunities across blockchain ecosystems.

Private Market Opportunities

We selectively evaluate first-round and private market opportunities where strong structural demand, sector tailwinds, or strategic positioning create attractive long-term upside.

Cross-Border Themes

The firm tracks opportunities shaped by capital flows, policy divergence, and sector leadership across the United States and Asian markets.
