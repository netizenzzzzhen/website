Investment Strategy

Our strategy is built on long-term capital appreciation, strict downside awareness, and selective participation in structural growth themes.

Core Approach

We apply a research-driven investment framework centered on business quality, earnings durability, valuation discipline, and risk asymmetry. We seek opportunities where long-term fundamentals can drive returns without relying purely on market multiple expansion.

Investment Philosophy

We believe long-term returns are primarily driven by earnings growth, capital allocation quality, and disciplined entry points. Our process emphasizes capital preservation first, followed by selective concentration in ideas with strong structural support.

Risk Discipline

  • Scenario-based review of upside, downside, and liquidity risk
  • Position sizing based on conviction and drawdown tolerance
  • Continuous reassessment of thesis durability and macro sensitivity
  • Preference for asymmetry over short-term headline momentum

Market Focus

  • United States listed markets
  • Asian listed equities and cross-border opportunities
  • Digital assets and blockchain-related ecosystems
  • Selective private and first-round investment opportunities
